This is important to know

Have you ever heard that discipline is stronger than motivation?
Motivation is a variable state, and discipline is a habit that, once developed, you will stick to for a long time regardless of the circumstances. The to-do list will help you build the necessary discipline. Write down the most important things to carry out the plan!

How to use the To-Do List?

Here are the steps:
1) Write the task in the input field and click the "Add task" button;
2) Click the "pencil button" to edit the file. Make the necessary changes and click the "update task" button to save them;
3) Click on the button at the beginning of the task to mark it as completed.
4) Click the "trash can button" to delete the task.
